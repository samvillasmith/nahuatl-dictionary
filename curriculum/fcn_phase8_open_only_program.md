# FCN Phase 8 — Open-only B2 Track Program

## Goal

Use only open and legally reusable sources already in FCN plus Nāhuatlahtolli to build the next proficiency layer:

- strong A1–A2
- real B1 conversation
- structured B2 pathway
- documentary C1 stretch in curated domains

## Open-only source backbone

- existing FCN lexicon DB
- Kaikki / Wiktionary-derived lexical rows
- Siméon and later Molina as public-domain reference layers
- UD grammar evidence
- Nāhuatlahtolli as the modern Huasteca lesson/dialogue backbone

## Phase 8 deliverables

1. ingest Nāhuatlahtolli lesson structure
2. attach lesson vocab to the FCN lexicon where possible
3. derive dialogue rows
4. derive pedagogical units
5. derive a construction bank
6. export curriculum-ready tables for A1–B1

## Instructional sequence

### Stage 1 — A1 foundation

- greetings
- identity
- family
- place
- routine actions
- simple wants and needs
- yes/no and information questions

### Stage 2 — A2 interaction

- daily routine
- food and home
- directions
- shopping and requests
- time and scheduling
- short narration

### Stage 3 — B1 conversation

- work and school
- health and body
- explanation and reasons
- plans and intentions
- extended dialogue and roleplay
- paragraph-length narration

### Stage 4 — B2 pathway

- summarizing lessons and readings
- contrast and comparison
- multi-step explanation
- opinion with reasons
- public-facing interaction and presentation
- controlled essay and discourse production

## Confidence tiers

- `Open_attested` — directly harvested from open lesson material
- `Lexicon_aligned` — matches existing FCN lexical evidence
- `Editorial_normalized` — normalized in line with Phase 6 rules
- `Construction_candidate` — reusable teaching pattern awaiting curation

## Success metrics

### Minimum Phase 8 success

- 20+ lesson units
- 150+ dialogue utterances
- 500+ aligned lesson vocab items
- 200+ construction candidates
- 12 pedagogical units ready for editing

### B2-track success

- 24 full units
- 300+ dialogue utterances
- 350+ curated construction patterns
- stable A1–B2 curriculum scaffold
- learner can narrate, explain, compare, and sustain discussion in familiar domains

## What this phase does not claim

- speaker validation
- unrestricted native-like C1
- community-certified spoken standard

## What this phase does claim

- legally open B2-oriented infrastructure
- real conversational EHN learning path
- executable curriculum architecture built on the existing FCN stack
