# Flor y Canto Nahuatl — Mission Statements

**Eastern Huasteca Nahuatl for Speech, Modern Standard Nahuatl for Writing**

Version: 0.1
Date: March 25, 2026

---

## One-Sentence Mission Statement

Flor y Canto Nahuatl exists to establish Eastern Huasteca Nahuatl as a teachable spoken standard, Modern Standard Nahuatl as a respected written norm, and a living poetic high register that reconnects Nahuatl speech, literature, song, and public life.

## One-Paragraph Mission Statement

Flor y Canto Nahuatl is a standardization, teaching, and literary project dedicated to building a coherent modern Nahuatl framework with Eastern Huasteca Nahuatl as its spoken base and Modern Standard Nahuatl as its written norm. Its purpose is to create durable linguistic infrastructure: a usable lexicon, a clear orthography, a teachable grammar, a respected public prose standard, and a poetic register capable of supporting songs, literature, ceremony, and modern cultural expression. FCN exists not to erase living Nahuatl diversity, but to provide one disciplined, transparent, and culturally serious pathway through which learners, writers, artists, and communities can speak, write, teach, and create in Nahuatl with continuity, dignity, and modern relevance.

## One-Page Mission Statement

Flor y Canto Nahuatl is the effort to give Nahuatl a modern framework equal to its depth, beauty, and historical importance. It is founded on a simple but demanding conviction: a language with a great literary past and a living present deserves a future that is structured, teachable, writable, and culturally powerful. FCN therefore takes on four tasks at once. It standardizes, it teaches, it builds literature, and it helps restore public continuity between speech, writing, and artistic expression.

At its spoken center, Flor y Canto Nahuatl adopts Eastern Huasteca Nahuatl as its pedagogical and communicative base. This gives the project a living, concrete, speakable foundation. FCN does not begin from abstraction, nor from an invented compromise detached from communities. It begins from a real speech tradition capable of grounding dialogue, pronunciation, everyday grammar, and modern use. The project's spoken standard exists so that a learner can actually speak, understand, and internalize Nahuatl as a living language rather than only as a textual object.

At its written center, FCN establishes Modern Standard Nahuatl as a disciplined reference norm. This written norm exists to make Nahuatl readable, teachable, searchable, publishable, and shareable across contexts. It is meant to support grammar, dictionaries, essays, subtitles, educational texts, public writing, and future literary development. It is not intended to erase dialects or replace community speech. Instead, it provides a stable written layer above the spoken base: a norm that can organize vocabulary, clarify orthography, connect registers, and make modern publication possible at scale.

At its artistic center, Flor y Canto Nahuatl recognizes that Nahuatl must not only be spoken and studied, but also sung, written, performed, and elevated. For that reason, FCN includes a poetic and classical high register within its system. This register gives writers, lyricists, and artists a way to draw from older Nahuatl literary power without confusing literary diction with everyday speech. In FCN, poetry is not a decorative afterthought. It is part of the language's civilizational continuity. Song, refrain, rhetorical parallelism, ceremonial address, and elevated expression all belong within the project's vision of a modern Nahuatl future.

Flor y Canto Nahuatl is therefore not merely a textbook initiative and not merely a preservation archive. It is infrastructure. It is a set of tools, standards, books, and editorial principles that make future work possible. It aims to produce a core lexicon, an orthography manual, a style guide, a grammar, a spoken course, a literary manual, and a living body of texts. It seeks to make Nahuatl easier to learn, easier to publish, easier to teach, easier to perform, and harder to marginalize.

The project is guided by discipline and transparency. It distinguishes clearly between spoken Eastern Huasteca Nahuatl, neutral written MSN, and poetic/classical high register. It labels sources, marks uncertainty, separates evidence from editorial decision, and refuses silent conflation of registers. It respects living speech, historical literature, and modern creativity by giving each its place inside one coherent framework.

Ultimately, Flor y Canto Nahuatl exists because a language of this magnitude should not be forced to choose between the village and the library, between the classroom and the song, between the archive and the future. FCN exists so that Nahuatl can be spoken with confidence, written with consistency, taught with clarity, and created with grandeur.

## Why This Exists (Website Text)

Nahuatl has extraordinary historical depth, living communities, and immense literary potential, but modern learners and writers still face a major practical problem: the path from speaking, to writing, to publishing, to creating in Nahuatl is fragmented. Flor y Canto Nahuatl exists to build that missing path.

This project establishes Eastern Huasteca Nahuatl as a teachable spoken base and Modern Standard Nahuatl as a shared written norm. It also preserves a place for a poetic and classical high register so that Nahuatl can live not only in conversation and instruction, but also in song, literature, ceremony, and public cultural expression.

Flor y Canto Nahuatl exists to create linguistic infrastructure: a core lexicon, a clear orthography, a transparent register system, a practical grammar, a spoken course, a literary manual, and texts that people can actually use. Its aim is not to erase living varieties or flatten Nahuatl into one artificial form. Its aim is to make one serious, disciplined pathway through the language so that speech, writing, teaching, and creation can reinforce one another.

In plain terms, this exists so that Nahuatl can be spoken clearly, written consistently, taught effectively, and created beautifully.
