# Phase 8.6 — Spoken EHN Primer Foundation

## Purpose
Pre-build the foundation for the eventual public `Spoken EHN Primer`.

## Deliverables
- primer-ready unit sequence
- beginner lesson skeleton
- dialogue shortlist
- drill shortlist
- grammar-note shortlist
- glossary starter
- contamination check against classical-only material

## Selection logic
- prioritize A1 + A2 first
- include early B1 bridge units selectively
- exclude anything marked classical-only or poetic-high from primer core
- prefer direct lesson/dialogue evidence over editorially abstract constructions

## Exit criteria
A coherent primer subset exists and can be turned into Deliverable 15 with minimal additional structural work.
